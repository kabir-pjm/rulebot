Rule-ID: 001
Topic: Travel Reimbursement
Policy: Employees must submit travel receipts within 15 days of their trip.
Details: Only economy class airfare is reimbursable. Business class is not allowed.

Rule-ID: 002
Topic: Leave Policy
Policy: Employees are entitled to 20 paid leave days per year.
Details: All leave must be approved by a line manager at least 2 weeks in advance.

Rule-ID: 003
Topic: Work From Home (WFH)
Policy: WFH is allowed for a maximum of 2 days per week for eligible staff.
Details: WFH days must be pre-scheduled and logged in the company portal.
